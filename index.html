Hi!

I'm Jerin Philip, MS by Research Student at Center for Visual Information Technology (CVIT), IIIT-Hyderabad, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. C.V. Jawahar and Prof. Vinay P. Namboodiri.

I worked on the OCR project in the past, trained a few machine translation models which made into leaderboards for tasks in Indian Languages. Of late, I have been looking into methods determining uncertainty in the predictions of neural models used in machine translation and its further applications.

I'm currently interning at NAVER LABS Europe supervised by Prof. Laurent Besacier and Alex Berard, where I investigate resource-efficient NMT models.
