Hi! I'm Jerin Philip.

Currently, I write software to enable deep-learning models to run on the average users' computers. Prior to this, I have 4 years of experience applying machine learning to solve problems involving image or text data - working across data-collection and cleaning, training and deployment. Most of my prior research is centered around machine translation.

I am fluent in modern C++, Python and interoperating both.
